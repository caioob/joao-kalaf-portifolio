## 3. Deviations from spec

1. **Hero is opted out via a `reveal` prop, not by living outside `Section`.** The plan assumed Hero rendered bare in `App`; it actually composes `Section > Container` like every other section (correctly — it keeps the shared rhythm). Adding `reveal={false}` is the smaller, more consistent change than unwrapping it.

## 4. Issues found & fixed

- **jsdom has neither `IntersectionObserver` nor `matchMedia`** — `useInView` needs both. Added minimal mocks in `src/test/setup.js` (IO reports everything immediately in view; `matchMedia` reports no reduced-motion preference). Production code untouched; both APIs ship in every target browser.

## 5. Decisions of record

- **Progressive-enhancement reveal over a CSS-only `@starting-style` or an always-hidden-then-JS approach** — guarantees content is never trapped invisible by a JS or IO failure, satisfying the "motion never required" a11y rule directly in the hook rather than relying on the global kill switch alone.
- **Hit targets:** filter pills/toggle segments sit slightly under the 44px touch target the design doc cites for touch; left as-is to preserve the calm inline rhythm (they exceed the WCAG 2.1 AA 24px minimum). Flagged here rather than silently resized — revisit if the designer reports touch friction.
